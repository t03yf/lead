How the System Works

Six core modules handle the complete lead lifecycle. Each one solves a specific operational failure.

1. Lead Capture Engine

Ingests leads from any source: forms, APIs, webhooks, email parsing. Standardizes data format regardless of where it comes from.

Why it exists: You're using multiple lead sources. Each one formats data differently. This normalizes everything so the rest of the system works consistently.

2. Qualification & Scoring Router

Applies your qualification criteria automatically. Scores leads, assigns priority levels, routes to the right person or team.

Why it exists: Manual qualification burns time and introduces inconsistency. This applies the same logic to every lead, instantly, without human judgment calls.

3. CRM Integration Layer

Syncs leads into your CRM in real-time. Creates records, updates fields, maintains data integrity across systems.

Why it exists: Leads that don't make it into your CRM don't exist. This ensures every lead is tracked from minute one, with full context.

4. Intelligent Notification System

Sends alerts to the right people via Slack, SMS, or email based on lead priority and routing rules. High-priority leads trigger immediate notifications.

Why it exists: Shared inboxes don't work. This ensures the right person knows about the right lead within seconds of submission.

5. Automated Response & Scheduling

Sends immediate acknowledgment emails, offers calendar booking links, triggers follow-up sequences. All within 60 seconds of form submission.

Why it exists: Speed matters. An instant, personalized response keeps leads warm while your team prepares actual outreach. This buys you time without looking slow.

6. Monitoring & Error Detection

Tracks every lead through the system. Logs all actions. Flags failures immediately. Sends alerts if something breaks.

Why it exists: You need to know when the system fails before leads are lost. This gives you full visibility into what's working and what's not.
